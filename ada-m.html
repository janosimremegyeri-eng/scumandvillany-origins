Unit: ADA(m)

Adaptive Diagnostic Assistant (mobile)

A field diagnostic and assistance unit — partially autonomous and useful in repairs and data recovery.

Background

One of several ADA units produced for industrial maintenance. This particular unit was reprogrammed and freed from corporate oversight; it now travels with the crew.

Playbook

Diagnostic assistant — handles scans, triage, data reads, and some field repairs.

Traits

  • Analytical and literal
  • Curious about organic behavior
  • Limited autonomy (no Urbot core)

Gear & Notes

Integrated sensor array, extendable manipulator arm, secure memory vault. ADA(m) stores crew medical logs and maintenance blueprints on memory crystals.

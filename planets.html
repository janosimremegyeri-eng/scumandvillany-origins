PROCYON SECTOR

Remote resource frontier of the Hegemony

The Procyon Sector contains four star systems connected by jumpgates. Colonized over a century ago by Noble House Nim-Amar and now overseen by House Malklaith, it is a distant, resource-rich region governed by the 51st Legion and a local System Police force.

Sector Facts
  • Systems 4
  • Major authorities House Malklaith, Engineers, Counters, Sah'iir
  • Primary role Resources & industry

Rin System

The Rin system is the gateway to Procyon and its administrative hub, with jumpgates to the Core, Holt, and Iota. It is the first stop for newcomers and the location of the sector's key institutions.

Aleph

Aleph is a toxic, tectonically unstable mining world. Its surface is scarred by extraction operations as the Hegemony takes every valuable mineral it can from the planet.

Warren

Warren is a futuristic ecumenopolis moon where House Malklaith, the Counters Guild, and the Sah'iir maintain offices. It serves as the sector's political and economic control center.

Vet

Vet is a gas giant mined by the SB-176 station, the local Guild of Engineers headquarters. Its thick atmosphere conceals industrial platforms and resource-harvesting infrastructure.

Baftoma

Baftoma has been stripped almost bare by the Hegemony, leaving only bare rock scaffolding. It stands as a warning about the cost of relentless extraction.

Holt System

The Holt system was second to be colonized but remains largely underdeveloped. Its worlds are still finding their place in the sector's hierarchy.

Sonhandra

Sonhandra is tidally locked, with life clinging to the narrow twilight terminator. Habitats and travel corridors must stay in the permanent dusk between burning day and freezing night.

Mem

Mem is an ocean world home to the native Memish species, whose presence was hidden from the Hegemony for a century after colonization. It remains remote and culturally distinct.

Vos / Glimmer

Vos, also called Glimmer, is covered in graphite and diamond, with access tightly regulated by the Guild of Engineers. It is a high-security resource world, and the sealed Hantu gate there remains an unsolved mystery.

Iota System

The Iota system is the industrial heart of Procyon. Its worlds produce goods, pharmaceuticals, and ship components for the rest of the sector.

Amerath

Amerath is a garden planet and the main hub of pharmaceutical research and production. Its greener environment contrasts with the sector's harsher industrial worlds.

Indri

Indri is covered entirely by industrial areas and wastelands. About 25% of the sector's goods are manufactured here, supported by shipyards in the nearby asteroid belts.

Lithios

Lithios is an ice planet mined for water and liquefied gases. It is also famous for the ice palaces left by an extinct native civilization.

Brekk System

Brekk is the cultural center of Procyon, known for education and the arts. Its worlds are less about raw extraction and more about prestige and knowledge.

Shimaya

Shimaya is a desert planet where the Khalud Academy trains the sector's elite scholars. Sand and scholarship blend in a harsh but prestigious environment.

Nightfall

Nightfall is the most Earth-like world in Procyon, with 13 moons causing frequent eclipses. It is famed for haute cuisine, theater, and the Obelisk station military base.

Aketi

Aketi is a jungle planet with extremely aggressive fauna. Settlement has been abandoned, and the world is now treated as a protected nature preserve.
